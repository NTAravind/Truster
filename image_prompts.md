# TRUSTER - Image Generation Prompts

These prompts are tailored to generate highly detailed flat vector illustrations in a monochromatic duotone blue color palette, matching the reference style.

**💡 Pro-Tip for Image Generation:** 
If the generator gives you 3D or overly realistic results, add negative prompts (if supported by your tool) like: `--no 3d, realistic, photography, shadows, gradients, multicolored`

---

## 1. Trust Code Solutions (Business Systems & ERP)
**Prompt:** 
> A highly detailed flat vector illustration collage representing enterprise business software and consulting. Monochromatic duotone blue color palette using shades of royal blue, light blue, and crisp white. Clean dark blue outlines, subtle halftone and stippling shading textures. The composition includes overlapping elements: a modern laptop displaying data dashboards, floating financial charts and bar graphs, interlocking mechanical gears, a professional clipboard with a checklist, interconnected network nodes, and a sleek modern office building in the background. Arranged as a cohesive cluster with a cutout sticker feel, professional corporate vector art style, clean background.
